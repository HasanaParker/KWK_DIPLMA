DIPLMA gives advice and valuable resources to disadvantaged students. The level of education a student will receive in the US varies from state to state and even from school to school. The wealth gap in America furthers the education gap, leaving many aspiring students without proper guidance. DIPLMA is a website that aims to bridge that gap by providing students with beneficial information and advice.
